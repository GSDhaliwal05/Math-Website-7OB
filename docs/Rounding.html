Understanding Rounding

Learn why and how rounding is used in mathematics.

Why Rounding is Done

Rounding is used to simplify numbers, making them easier to work with. It helps in estimating values and is useful in everyday situations, such as when dealing with money or measurements.

How Rounding is Done

To round a number, follow these steps:

  • Identify the place value to which you want to round (e.g., nearest whole number, tenth, hundredth).
  • Look at the digit immediately to the right of that place value.
  • If that digit is 5 or greater, round up. If itâ€™s less than 5, round down.
