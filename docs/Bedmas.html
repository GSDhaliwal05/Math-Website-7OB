BEDMAS

What is BEDMAS?

BEDMAS is an acronym that helps remember the order of operations in mathematics:

  • B: Brackets
  • E: Exponents
  • D: Division
  • M: Multiplication
  • A: Addition
  • S: Subtraction

Worked Example

Calculate the following expression: \(3 + 6 \times (5 + 4) \div 3 - 7\).

Step-by-Step:

1. Solve inside the brackets: \(5 + 4 = 9\).

2. Now, the expression is \(3 + 6 \times 9 \div 3 - 7\).

3. Next, perform multiplication and division from left to right:

6 Ã— 9 = 54, then \(54 \div 3 = 18\).

4. Now, the expression is \(3 + 18 - 7\).

5. Finally, perform addition and subtraction from left to right: \(3 + 18 = 21\) and \(21 - 7 = 14\).

Final Answer: 14
