BEDMAS

BEDMAS explained

BEDMAS stands for brackets, exponents, division and multiplication (from left to right), addition and subtraction (from left to right).

Example

For example, in the expression \(8 + 2 \times 5\), you would first do the multiplication:

Answer: \(8 + 10 = 18\)

Practice problems

1. Solve: \(3 + 6 \times (5 + 4)\)

Answer: \(3 + 6 \times 9 = 57\)

2. Solve: \(8 \times 2^2 - 3\)

Answer: \(8 \times 4 - 3 = 29\)

3. Solve: \(5 + (2 + 3) \times 4\)

Answer: \(5 + 5 \times 4 = 25\)
